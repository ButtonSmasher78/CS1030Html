Apple-1 and Apple-2

What is Apple-1 and Apple-2

The Apple-1 and Apple-2 were one of the first computer released by Apple. These computers help Apple stay afloat in the 1980s and 90s. These computer changed how people used them and how they can be useful.

Apple-1

The Apple-1 was release in 1976 and only 62 of them exist. It was just a board and keyboard so you require to have a television set to use. The computer was sold to business because they weren't as expensive as other computers.

Apple-2

The Apple-2 was released in 1977 and was like the Apple-1 but with more expandability and improved. This computer was one of the first to be marketed as a personal computer. The computer had everything built in so you didn't need anything else. They were sold to familys and schools which had kids be more interested in computers. The motherboard had slots that could be expanded on to make the computer better or to connect to external devices.

Why were they important

  • Brought more people to use computer
  • Made Apple be one of the biggest tech companys
